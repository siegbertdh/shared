Discovery notes
How It Works

Intelligent context,
delivered on arrival

Before the technician reaches the site, Einstein has already pulled, analysed and summarised everything relevant to today's job.

📋

Work Order Intelligence

Einstein cross-references similar historical jobs — repair patterns, failure modes and previous resolutions surface automatically.

📡

IoT Signal Analysis

Live sensor data from the Uptime Center feeds the brief. Pressure drops, vibration spikes and temperature anomalies flagged with context.

🎧

Hands-Free Audio

The brief is listenable via text-to-speech — technicians absorb it while suiting up in Chemical PPE, without touching the screen.

🏭

On-Site Fleet Visibility

All assets at the customer location listed for quick visual inspection — dryers, generators, pumps — proactively surfaced.

🔧

Parts Pre-Loaded

Recommended parts listed before the visit starts. No mid-job calls to the warehouse, no second visits for missing parts.

⚠️

Skill-Aware Guidance

Einstein checks technician certification. Expired certifications trigger warnings and surface the right documentation automatically.
