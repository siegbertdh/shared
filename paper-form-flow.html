Process
How it works
1

Upload a photo or scan

Take a photo of the paper form — a refrigerant log, JSA safety checklist, PM inspection form, whatever the customer uses. Upload it directly in Cursor.

2

AI extracts every field

Gemini Vision reads the form image and identifies every field — labels, types, validation rules, conditional sections. You review and confirm the extraction before anything gets built.

3

Fields get mapped to components

Each field maps to the right Data Capture component automatically — text inputs, picklists, signature fields, number steppers, photo capture, repeater sections. Conditional logic gets wired in too.

4

Flow is built, wired, deployed

Cursor generates the full Data Capture flow and deploys it to the Salesforce org via CLI. The flow is linked to Work Orders and ready for FSL Mobile — including offline mode.

The difference it makes

Before

Half a day, manually

Recreating a paper form as a Data Capture flow meant manually adding each field, picking components, configuring logic, and wiring to Work Orders. Repetitive, error-prone, and time-consuming for every customer.

After

Same session, same meeting

Upload the form, confirm the extraction, watch it deploy. The customer can see their actual form running on mobile in the same session where they described the problem. That's a different kind of conversation.
